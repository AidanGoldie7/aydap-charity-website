Our Project

Learn More About the Project

The school is owned and managed by our "implementing partner" - African Youth Development Action Project which is a NGO registered in Liberia.

That part of Liberia has been described as being in abject poverty and a sense of hopelessness and we are greatly encouraged by the progress made to date (2023) with limited funds available.

Other funding has been provided from other UK sources and personal donations; and an increasing support is being developed from inside Libera including the local District Education Officer, a cocoa plantation, and other Liberian potential funders.
